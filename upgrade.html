Expert Advice on O2 Upgrades, Eligibility & Deals

When Can You Upgrade Your O2 Phone?

One of the most common questions we receive is: "When am I eligible to upgrade?" The answer depends on several factors, including the type of plan you're on, how long you've had your current device, and whether you're willing to pay any early upgrade fees.

Standard Upgrade Eligibility

For most O2 customers on traditional 24-month contracts, you'll become eligible for a standard upgrade once you've completed the minimum term of your agreement:

  • 24-month contracts: Eligible for upgrade after 24 months
  • 36-month contracts: Eligible for upgrade after 36 months
  • O2 Refresh plans: Eligible to upgrade once your Device Plan is paid off

O2 Refresh: The Flexible Upgrade Option

O2 Refresh has revolutionised how customers upgrade their phones. Unlike traditional contracts where you're locked in for the full term, O2 Refresh separates your bill into two parts: the Device Plan (the cost of your phone) and the Airtime Plan (your minutes, texts, and data).

With O2 Refresh, you can upgrade as soon as you've paid off your Device Plan. This could be:

  • At the end of your original term (typically 24 or 36 months)
  • Earlier if you choose to pay off your remaining Device Plan balance
  • Anytime by trading in your current device to offset the remaining balance
O2 Refresh Example: Sarah has had her iPhone 13 for 18 months on a 36-month O2 Refresh plan. Her Device Plan balance is £240, and O2 values her iPhone 13 at £280 trade-in. She wants to upgrade to an iPhone 15. Her trade-in covers her remaining balance, and she receives £40 credit towards her new Device Plan!

How to Check Your O2 Upgrade Eligibility

Method 1: Using the My O2 App

  1. Download and open the My O2 app on your smartphone
  2. Log in using your O2 account credentials
  3. Navigate to the "Upgrade" or "My Device" section
  4. Your eligibility status will be displayed clearly

Method 2: Online via O2 Website

  1. Visit o2.co.uk and sign in to your account
  2. Go to "My Account" or "My O2"
  3. Look for the "Upgrade" option in your dashboard

O2 Trade-In Programme

The O2 trade-in programme is one of the best ways to reduce the cost of your upgrade. By trading in your current device, you can offset your remaining Device Plan balance, reduce the cost of your new phone, or even receive credit towards your bill.

How O2 Trade-In Works

  1. Get a valuation: Use O2's online tool or speak to our team to find out what your device is worth
  2. Accept the offer: If you're happy with the valuation, accept the trade-in
  3. Send your device: O2 provides a pre-paid postage pack for you to return your old phone
  4. Device inspection: O2 checks your device matches the condition you stated
  5. Receive credit: The trade-in value is applied to your account or new device purchase

Tips for Getting the Best O2 Upgrade Deal

1. Time Your Upgrade Strategically

Certain times of year offer better upgrade deals:

  • Black Friday (November): Some of the year's best deals
  • Boxing Day/January Sales: Excellent discounts on previous models
  • New Phone Launches: Older models see price drops

2. Compare All Available Options

Don't just accept the first offer you see. Compare different device storage capacities, contract lengths, and plan tiers.

3. Maximise Your Trade-In Value

  • Keep your current phone in excellent condition
  • Use a case and screen protector from day one
  • Trade in before your device loses significant value

4. Don't Overlook the Total Cost

When comparing deals, calculate the total cost over the contract term. A lower monthly cost over a longer period may actually cost more overall.
