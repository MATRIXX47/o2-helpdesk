# Comprehensive SEO Strategy for O2 Helpdesk Support Website (UK)
## Independent Customer Support Service - Not Affiliated with O2

---

## Executive Summary

This SEO strategy targets UK customers seeking help with O2 mobile services. The strategy focuses on high-commercial-intent keywords, question-based long-tail queries, and comprehensive topic coverage to establish authority and drive organic traffic that converts into AdSense revenue.

**Key Opportunity**: O2 has significant customer service challenges with average wait times of 3-4 minutes, low satisfaction ratings (1.8/5), and common issues across billing, technical support, and account management. An independent helpdesk can capture this frustrated search traffic.

## 7. INTERNAL LINKING STRATEGY

### Hub-and-Spoke Model

**Hub Pages** (High Authority, Comprehensive):
- /billing/ - Links to all billing sub-pages
- /technical/ - Links to all technical sub-pages
- /account/ - Links to all account sub-pages

**Spoke Pages** (Specific Issues):
- Link back to hub page
- Link to 2-3 related spoke pages
- Link to relevant blog posts

### Contextual Linking Rules

1. **Every article should have**:
   - 1 link to parent category (hub)
   - 2-3 links to related articles
   - 1 link to relevant tool/resource

2. **Anchor Text Guidelines**:
   - Use descriptive, keyword-rich anchors
   - Vary anchor text (don't over-optimize)
   - Example: "If you're struggling with [high bills](/billing/bill-too-high/), you may also want to learn about [setting a spending cap](/account/spending-cap/)."

3. **Navigation Links**:
   - Breadcrumb on all pages
   - "Related Issues" sidebar
   - "Popular Articles" footer section

### Link Distribution Strategy

```
Homepage (100% link equity)
    ├── Category Hubs (20% each)
    │   ├── Sub-pages (5% each)
    │   └── Related sub-pages (cross-link)
    ├── Blog Posts (10% total)
    │   └── Link to relevant hubs
    └── Tools/Resources (10%)
        └── Link to relevant hubs
```
