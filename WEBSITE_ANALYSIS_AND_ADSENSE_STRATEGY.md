### How to Be 100% Google Policy Safe

#### Rule 1: NEVER Do These (Instant Ban Risk)
- Never ask visitors to click ads ("support us by clicking")
- Never place ads on pages with no real content
- Never use more than 3-4 ads per page (for new sites)
- Never place ads that look like navigation or content
- Never use pop-up or pop-under ads
- Never buy fake traffic or use click bots
- Never copy content from other sites
- Never place ads on legal pages (privacy, terms, cookies)

#### Rule 2: ALWAYS Do These (Policy Compliance)
- Always label ad areas clearly (small "Advertisement" text above)
- Always maintain 70%+ content-to-ad ratio
- Always keep ads 150+ pixels from clickable content on mobile
- Always have cookie consent before loading ad scripts
- Always disclose ad partnerships in your privacy policy
- Always use responsive ad units for mobile
- Always keep content above the fold (ads below hero)

#### Rule 3: Trademark Safety (Specific to Your Site)
Since you use "O2" in your domain and content, you MUST:
- Keep the "independent service" disclaimer prominent on EVERY page
- Never use O2 official logos
- Never imply you ARE O2 or are endorsed by O2
- Use phrases like "support FOR O2 customers" not "O2 support"
- Add a trademark notice section to your Terms page

#### Rule 4: Claims & Statistics Safety
Your current stats need attention:
| Current Claim | Risk | Safe Alternative |
|---------------|------|-----------------|
| "50K+ Customers Helped" | HIGH - unverifiable | "Helping O2 customers since 2018" |

### Best Performing Ad Positions (Based on Google Data)

| Bottom of mobile screen (anchor) | 80%+ | MEDIUM | Yes |

### Ad Format Recommendations

| Format | Size | Where to Use | Expected CTR |
|--------|------|-------------|-------------|
| Responsive Display | Auto | In-article, between sections | 1-3% |
| Large Rectangle | 336x280 | Mid-content, end of content | 1.5-3% |
| Leaderboard | 728x90 | Between sections (desktop) | 0.5-1.5% |
| Mobile Banner | 320x100 | Between sections (mobile) | 1-2% |
| Multiplex | Fluid | End of articles (related content style) | 2-4% |
| In-feed | Fluid | Blog listing page | 1-2% |

### Revenue Optimisation Tips
1. **Enable Auto Ads initially** - Let Google find optimal positions, then fine-tune
2. **Use responsive units** - They maximise fill rate across all devices
3. **Reserve ad space with min-height CSS** - Prevents CLS (layout shift) penalty
4. **Never block ad categories unless necessary** - More competition = higher CPM
5. **Test desktop vs mobile separately** - Mobile needs different placements
6. **Monitor Core Web Vitals** - Poor scores reduce ad rates by up to 50%
