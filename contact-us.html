What to Have Ready When You Call

Preparing before your call helps us resolve your issue faster. While not every item on this list will apply to every situation, having relevant information to hand can significantly speed up the diagnosis and resolution process.

📋 Essential Information Checklist

  • ☐
    Your Account or Company Name

    This helps us pull up your service history and configuration details immediately

  • ☐
    A Description of the Problem

    What isn't working? When did it start? Has anything changed recently?

  • ☐
    Error Messages (Exact Text)

    Write down or photograph any error messages exactly as they appear

  • ☐
    Device/Make Model Information

    Computer model, operating system version, software versions affected

  • ☐
    Previous Case Reference (If Applicable)

    If this relates to an ongoing issue, your reference number helps us connect the dots

  • ☐
    Your Availability for Follow-Up

    When are you available for remote access or an on-site visit if needed?

💡 Pro Tip

If you're experiencing a technical issue right now, try to call from a different device or phone. This allows us to guide you through troubleshooting steps on the affected machine while staying connected.
