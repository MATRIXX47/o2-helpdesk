O2 Voicemail Setup: Complete Guide

📅 January 3, 2025 🏷️ Technical ⏱️ 8 min read

Voicemail is an essential feature that ensures you never miss important calls when you're unavailable. This complete guide covers everything you need to know about setting up and managing your O2 voicemail service.

Setting Up O2 Voicemail for the First Time

If you've never used O2 voicemail before, here's how to get started:

Step 1: Activate Voicemail

  1. Dial 901 from your O2 mobile
  2. Follow the voice prompts to set up your voicemail
  3. Create a secure PIN (4-6 digits)
  4. Record your personal greeting

Step 2: Choose Your Greeting

You have several greeting options:

  • Personal greeting: Record your own message
  • Standard greeting: "The person you are calling is not available..."
  • Name greeting: "[Your name] is not available..."

Accessing Your Voicemail

From Your O2 Phone

  • Dial 901
  • Or press and hold the "1" key
  • Enter your PIN when prompted

From Another Phone

  1. Dial your O2 mobile number
  2. Wait for voicemail to answer
  3. Press * (star) during the greeting
  4. Enter your O2 mobile number
  5. Enter your PIN

From Abroad

  1. Dial +44 7802 090901
  2. Enter your O2 mobile number
  3. Enter your PIN
Roaming Charges: Accessing voicemail from abroad may incur roaming charges. Consider using Wi-Fi calling or asking callers to text instead.

Voicemail Menu Options

Once you've accessed your voicemail, you'll hear the main menu:

PressFunction
1Listen to messages
2Change folders (new, saved, deleted)
3Greeting options
4Personal options (PIN, etc.)
5Send a message to another O2 user
0Help
*Return to main menu
#End call

Managing Your Messages

While Listening to a Message

  • Press 1: Replay message
  • Press 2: Save message
  • Press 3: Delete message
  • Press 4: Reply to message (O2 to O2 only)
  • Press 5: Forward message
  • Press 6: Hear message details (time, date)
  • Press 7: Skip back 5 seconds
  • Press 8: Pause/Resume
  • Press 9: Skip forward 5 seconds

Troubleshooting Common Voicemail Issues

"Voicemail Not Set Up" Error

If you hear this message when calling your own number:

  1. Dial 901 to activate voicemail
  2. Follow the setup prompts
  3. Test by calling your number from another phone

Forgotten PIN

If you've forgotten your voicemail PIN:

  1. Dial 901
  2. When asked for your PIN, press * (star)
  3. Follow the prompts to reset your PIN

Voicemail Not Picking Up

If calls aren't going to voicemail:

  1. Check call forwarding settings on your phone
  2. Ensure voicemail is activated (dial 901)
  3. Contact O2 if problems persist
